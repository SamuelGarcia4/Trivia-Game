Trivia Game

Rules

There are 5 questions, you have 50 seconds to answer them.

1. How many months have 28 days?





2. You walk a mile south, a mile east, and a mile north, and end up back where you started. You see a bear, what color is the bear?





3. If a red house is made of red bricks, and a blue house is made of blue bricks, what is a greenhouse made out of?





4. If there are 12 fish and half of them drown, how many are left?





5. If there is a basket of 13 apples and you take three of them, how many apples do you have?





The Answers!

Question 1: The correct answer is the 12.

Question 2: The correct answer is the White.

Question 3: The correct answer is the Glass.
